About Me

Hi, I'm Tuncay Akcasu, originally from Turkey and now based in London. I have a deep passion for beekeeping and natural products. At London Honey Works, I focus on producing high-quality bee products such as honey, pollen, propolis, and bee venom, while emphasizing sustainability and natural practices.

Hobbies

Besides beekeeping, I have a variety of interests that revolve around nature and creativity. Here are some of my favorite hobbies:

  • Beekeeping and learning about bee behavior
  • Exploring natural products and their benefits
  • Gardening and growing herbs
  • Outdoor activities and hiking in the countryside

Bee Products

At London Honey Works, I produce a range of natural bee products that are not only healthy but also support the environment:

  • Honey: 100% pure, raw, and unfiltered honey with a rich flavor.
  • Pollen: A great source of protein and essential nutrients.
  • Propolis: A natural substance with powerful antioxidant properties.
  • Bee Venom: Used in natural therapies and skincare products.
